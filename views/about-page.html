About

If Matt Hanley were to hand you a business card you would find out that he is a physical therapist who works for the VA. However that's only a small fraction of who Matt is in his entirety. Professionally Matt has also been a welder, a bartender, a playwright, a bike messenger and so much more. But to know who Matt is... you really just have to meet him.

He is an extremely engaging, thoughtful, intelligent, boisterous and good natured force to be reckoned with. He is as passionate about his work as he is about the migratory patterns of the local birds. He is as likely to quote Shakespeare as he is to quote one of the hosts of ESPN's Pardon the Interruption. Talking with Matt is less like having a conversation, and more like asking for the time and as a response being handed a masterful painting of the sky with the sun in its exact position. In other words, Matt is full of life and spending time with him fills you with life in return.
